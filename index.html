Mart Robarios Peña also known as em_X_pe (Artist Name) is a 27 yr. old. Born on October 29, 1995. Son of Mrs. Zenaida Lopez Robarios and Mr. Jesus Diñega Peña in town of Ligao province of Albay. He had five sister and 4 brother. He graduated high school in Ligao National High School (Ligao City, Albay) in year 2014. Due to lack of financial he decided to stop and work to help his parents.

After of 5 years working he decided to continue his study. He enrolled to Parañaque National High School - Main in Senior High to due the changes in curriculum. After he graduated senior high school in year 2021 he take a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at Polytechnic University of the Philippines - Parañaque Campus.

Mart loves taking pictures using his smartphone. His subject mostly is about the nature through this he express his loves from nature. He also love to paint and create beautiful masterpiece from his precious hands.
